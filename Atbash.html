The Atbash Cipher

The Atbash Cipher is a cipher where the albhabet is simply reveresed, each letter is replaced witht the corresponding letter 'ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ' becomes 'ZYXWVUTSRQPONMLKJIHGFEDCBA'. This cipher was originally used to encode the Hebrew alphabet. The name derives from the first, last, second, and second to last Hebrew letters (Aleph-Tav-Beth-Shin).
